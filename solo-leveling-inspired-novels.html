12 Best Solo Leveling Inspired Novels

Expert recommendations for discerning readers.

📅 ���� 7, 2026⏰ 8 min read
⚔️ NovelPick

Solo Leveling redefined what a power fantasy could be. Sung Jinwoo rise from the weakest hunter to a literally one-of-a-kind being struck a chord with millions of readers worldwide. If you have finished the manhwa and are hungry for more, these novels deliver the same visceral satisfaction of watching an underdog become unstoppable.

LitRPG and Progression Fantasy Gems

Webnovel | 2019 | 3000+ chapters

1. Shadow Slave by biotran

The gold standard for Solo Leveling fans. Protagonist starts with barely any combat ability and must grow through brutal training and dungeon crawling. The shadow-slave system echoes Sung Jinwoo own powers, and the pacing is relentless.

Royal Road | 2020 | 800+ chapters

2. The Legendary Mechanic by Chocolion

The protagonist transmigrates into a game world as an NPC and must level up mechanics instead of combat skills. Unique system, clever protagonist, and genuinely funny moments alongside the action.

Amazon | 2022 | 4 books

3. Primal Hunter by Zogarth

A gamer is reincarnated into a world where levels and skills are real. The protagonist has a unique ability that everyone underestimates, and watching him exploit it is deeply satisfying.

Royal Road | 2019 | 600+ chapters

4. Defiance of the Fall by JFbrinkman

A survival LitRPG where the apocalypse brings dungeon-like challenges. The protagonist is cunning, ruthless, and willing to do whatever it takes to grow stronger.

Webnovel | 2022 | 1800+ chapters

5. Super Gene Optimization Bath 9 by PhantomR

A man who was about to die gets a second chance through gene optimization. The systematic improvement, stat-by-stat growth, and power escalation will feel very familiar to Solo Leveling fans.

Amazon | 2021 | 3 books

6. All the Skills by Namo

A deck-building LitRPG where every skill drawn is a permanent choice. Strategic combat, clever combinations, and a protagonist who thinks several moves ahead.

Royal Road | 2020 | 400+ chapters

7. Infinite Stuck by SeventhStar

Trapped in a time loop with only the protagonist remembering. He uses each loop to grow stronger, uncover conspiracies, and prepare for an inevitable boss fight.

Webnovel | 2021 | 1200+ chapters

8. Dark Crowneans by SunnyG

An outcast noble in a magical world discovers his powers grow by consuming monster cores. Dark, brutal, and relentless. The progression is addictive.

Amazon | 2023 | 2 books

9. Blood Luna by Stephanie K. Parker

A werewolf who was supposed to be the weakest in her pack discovers her bloodline carries something far more dangerous. Solo Leveling energy with a female protagonist.

Royal Road | 2021 | 500+ chapters

10. The Rookie Executive by Zahard

Corporate cubicle worker gets thrown into a dungeon world where his accounting skills become literal superpower. Unique premise, excellent world-building.

Webnovel | 2020 | 1500+ chapters

11. Top Tier Providence by Fruit of Devotion

Mocked as the weakest spiritualist, the protagonist secretly accumulates legendary-tier cultivation techniques. A comedy of errors where his supposed weakness masks overwhelming power.

Webnovel | 2021 | 2000+ chapters

12. A Record of a Mortal Journey to Immortality by Pluto

Slow-burn progression fantasy where a mortal cultivator claws their way through a hostile world. Systematic power growth mirrors Solo Leveling spirit despite being cultivation rather than LitRPG.

Why Solo Leveling Fans Love These

The appeal is watching someone who started at the absolute bottom claw their way to the top through pure determination and strategic growth. Every book on this list delivers that same feeling: you are riding alongside a character who refuses to stay down.

Our Verdict

Shadow Slave is the closest thing to a spiritual successor. The Legendary Mechanic offers the best alternative system. Primal Hunter is the most straightforward LitRPG pleasure read. Each is worth your time.
