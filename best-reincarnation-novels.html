Best Reincarnation Novels

Second Chances, Rewritten Fates, and Lives Worth Living Twice

⚔️ LitRPG

Best Reincarnation Novels

The reincarnation fantasy is fundamentally about regret. We have all thought about what we would do if we could relive our lives ?? and these novels explore that fantasy in ways that are both escapist and surprisingly emotional. Whether the protagonist is gaming the system or simply trying to live better, the genre delivers something uniquely satisfying.

The Reincarnation Novel Hall of Fame

Webnovel | 2018 | 3000+ chapters

1. Trash of the Count Family by Yoo Ryeo Han

The protagonist wakes up in the body of a minor villain in a novel he once read. Rather than fight the plot, he quietly accumulates power and makes allies. The most intelligent "isekai" novel ever written. Cale Henituse is one of the most beloved protagonists in the genre.

Amazon | 2020 | 3 books

2. Beware of Chicken by casualfarmer

A cultivation novel protagonist transmigrates and decides to just... farm. And be nice to people. The most wholesome subversion of the power-fantasy genre, with genuine warmth and excellent character work.

Webnovel | 2021 | 1500+ chapters

3. Second Life Ranker by Sadoyeon

A brother seeks revenge for his murdered sibling by climbing the ranker tower that mirrors the achievements of his dead twin. Dark, revenge-driven, and relentless in its pacing.

Royal Road | 2022 | 600+ chapters

4. Vigor Mortis by Sina R. Alex

A gay man dies and wakes up as the dead heir of a powerful family in a world where homosexuality is accepted but political intrigue is deadly. The protagonist must navigate succession crises while hiding his true identity.

Amazon | 2021 | 2 books

5. The Wrong World by Vergebook

Reincarnation meets LitRPG in a story where the protagonist remembers his past life perfectly but the new world has slightly different rules. He exploits those differences systematically.

Webnovel | 2020 | 800+ chapters

6. My Vampire System by Jinzw

Wake up as a vampire in a world where vampires are second-class citizens. The system gives quests and rewards, and the protagonist uses both to become powerful while hiding his true nature.

Amazon | 2022 | Ongoing

7. The Mech Touch by Calaisian

Transmigrated into a space opera world with future tech and mechs. The protagonist designs custom mechs using knowledge from his past life and becomes legendary for building unconventional machines.

Royal Road | 2021 | 400+ chapters

8. KazEMa by Vera Kain

Transmigrated into the villainess of a dating sim and decides not to pursue any of the original romance routes ?? instead becoming a mentor to the protagonist original love interest. Clever subversion with genuinely touching moments.

What Makes Reincarnation Stories Work

The best reincarnation novels are not about the mechanics of rebirth ?? they are about character growth. The protagonist carries memories and regrets from a past life, and that weight of knowing better creates compelling tension. They know how to succeed, but using that knowledge without revealing themselves adds layers of intrigue.

Our Verdict

Trash of the Count Family for the gold standard of intelligent isekai. Beware of Chicken for the warmest, most satisfying subversion. Vigor Mortis for something genuinely different. Each represents reincarnation done right.
