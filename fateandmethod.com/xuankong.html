玄空飞星 · Flying Star Feng Shui

Xuankong — Flying Star Feng Shui

The most influential Feng Shui school — analyze how time and space interact to identify auspicious and inauspicious energy patterns in any building.

Xuankong (玄空飞星, "Mysterious Empty Flying Stars") is the most sophisticated and widely practiced school of Feng Shui in the modern world. Unlike simpler compass-based systems, Xuankong analyzes the interaction between time and space — treating every building as having a unique energy fingerprint determined by when it was built and its orientation relative to compass directions. This dynamic approach explains why the same floor plan produces different effects for different people and different eras.

The Flying Stars: Nine Energy Patterns

At the core of Xuankong are nine flying stars — numbered 1 through 9, each carrying distinct energetic qualities. These stars fly through the space of a building in a specific pattern determined by the building's facing direction and construction period. Each star's position determines the quality of energy in that area:

1
White Water
Wealth, career, potential
2
Black Illness
Health, illness, misfortune
3
Jade Wood
Dispute, travel, change
4
Green Wind
Study, creativity, romance
5
Yellow Disaster
Instability, delay, obstacle
6
White Metal
Authority, external help, travel
7
Red Loss
Financial loss, verbal dispute
8
White Earth
Prosperity, stability, unexpected gain
9
Purple Fire
Expansion, fame, succession

The 9-Grid: Your Floor Plan Energy Map

Xuankong divides any space into a 3×3 grid — nine zones corresponding to life areas (wealth, relationships, career, health, etc.). Each zone carries a flying star that changes annually and by 20-year period, meaning your home's energy shifts every year in subtle but measurable ways.

Time Dimension

Flying stars change based on era periods — a room's energy in 2026 differs from 2016. This is why Feng Shui adjustments should be updated annually.

Space Dimension

Direction and position in the building determine which star occupies each zone. The facing direction of the building activates different energy patterns.

Combination Effects

Stars combine with each other and with occupants' BaZi for compound effects — the same 7 star in a wealth area can mean financial loss or career advancement depending on configuration.

Why Xuankong Matters for Home and Business

A Xuankong analysis reveals which areas of your space are currently supportive and which carry challenge energy. Small adjustments — moving a desk, placing a water feature, adjusting a door — can shift how the flying star energy expresses itself. Annual updates are particularly important when entering a new era period (every 20 years), as the entire energy map shifts.

Key Applications

  • Home selection — evaluate a property's energy before buying or renting
  • Desk and bed placement — position yourself in favorable energy zones
  • Annual updates — adjust your space as flying stars shift each year
  • Business orientation — optimize the energy of key operational areas
  • Renovation timing — choose periods when energy supports construction changes
