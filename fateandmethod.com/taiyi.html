Taiyi Liuren

The ancient imperial numerology system — forecast major life events and identify auspicious timing at the highest level of Chinese metaphysics.

Taiyi Liuren (太乙神数, "Taiyi Divine Numbers") is the most ancient and esoteric of the classical Chinese numerology systems. For over two thousand years, it was reserved exclusively for the imperial court — used by emperors to analyze national fortunes, predict major political shifts, and identify the optimal timing for significant state actions. Of the "Three Great Systems" (三大数), Taiyi is considered the most abstract and cosmic in scope.

Cosmic Numerology: How Taiyi Works

Taiyi works by mapping the cyclical movement of major cosmic cycles against specific years and dates. It uses a complex system of positional numbers and cyclical transformations to identify patterns in the flow of major events — not individual daily questions, but the large-scale energetic currents that shape eras, nations, and generations.

Where Liuyao answers "what will happen?", Taiyi asks "what is the dominant cosmic trend right now, and where is it heading?" This makes it uniquely powerful for understanding the broader context within which individual events unfold.

Core Principles

Cosmic Cycles

Taiyi tracks the 360-year "cosmic cycle" and its subdivisions, identifying which phase of the larger pattern any given year occupies.

Positional Numbers

Numbers derived from time and date map to positional configurations that reveal the nature of the current cosmic energy.

Major Event Forecasting

Taiyi is used to predict significant political, economic, and natural events — not granular daily life questions.

Who Was Taiyi For?

Historically, only emperors and their designated advisors had access to Taiyi knowledge. The system was considered too powerful and too easily misused for general public application. Even today, Taiyi remains the least accessible of the classical systems — but its insights into large-scale energetic currents remain valid and valuable for those with the patience to study it.

Applications in the Modern Era

Modern practitioners use Taiyi to understand:

  • Era-level trends — what major energy is dominant in the current period and for the next decade
  • Year-level forecasts — what the primary cosmic focus of a specific year will be
  • Optimal timing — identifying days and periods when large-scale actions are most supported
  • National and institutional fortunes — understanding the larger cycles within which businesses and organizations operate

Taiyi is not a system for daily divination or specific question-answering. It is for those who want to understand the large-scale cosmic context — the tides beneath the surface of everyday events — that shapes the larger currents of history and life.
