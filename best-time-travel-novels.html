Why Time Travel Novels Captivate Us

At their core, time travel stories are about regret and hope. The ability to go back speaks to our universal wish to fix past mistakes. The ability to see the future speaks to our desperate need to prepare for what is coming.

What separates great time travel fiction from mediocre is how it handles the consequences. The best books in this genre understand that changing the past is never simple �� every intervention has costs, trade-offs, and unintended consequences that feel inevitable in hindsight.

Our Verdict
