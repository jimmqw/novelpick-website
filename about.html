About NovelPick

NovelPick curates the best web novels across every genre — cultivation, romance, LitRPG, xianxia, and more. Our recommendations are handpicked for reading quality, emotional impact, and narrative craft.

Our Selection Criteria

We evaluate novels on: pacing and plot structure, character depth, world-building coherence, translation quality (for non-English originals), and reader emotional engagement. We don't just list popular titles — we explain why each novel is worth your time.

What We Cover

Our coverage spans Royal Road, Webnovel, Wattpad, Qidian, Tapas, Kindle Vella, and Jupiter Gardens Press. From completed classics to ongoing serials with thousands of chapters, we find the gems across platforms.
